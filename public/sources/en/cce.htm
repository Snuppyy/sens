INTRODUCTION

Non-governmental non-commercial organization Information-educational «INTILISH» Centre (hereinafter organization) accepts this Code of conduct as the main document regulating the most important norms and rules for the staff-members of this organization.

GOALS AND TASKS OF THE CODE OF CONDUCTION

Code of conduction:

This is a document which presents the philosophy of NGO «INTILISH» RIEC and fundamental principles which form the base of corporative culture and ethics of organization, the observance of which is expected from all staff-members.

Code of conduct defines the following:

∑Voluntary self-regulation, provision of transparency and accountability;

∑Improvement of quality of services provided by NGO «INTILISH» RIEC , by means of acceptance of high standards of efficient processes of decision making;

∑Improvement of relationship between the community and different parties concerned.

The effect of the code is extended over all staff-members of organization including all affiliated branches of NGO «INTILISH» RIEC irrespectively of the rank and position of the staff-member in organization.

Mission of NGO «INTILISH» RIEC:

«Render assistance to those who strive to become better and to live better».

We dedicate part of our capabilities, means, talents and time to help the vulnerable population groups in overall upgrading and improvement of their live quality.

NGO «INTILISH» RIEC – is non-governmental non-commercial organization of «INTILISH» Information-educational Centre, it works for the improvement of the life quality of population and its vulnerable groups by means of preserving health, upgrading of the culture and educational levels, social adaptation. Vulnerable population groups includes but are not limited by people who are under risk, or who suffer from infectious, endemic, psychiatric, narcological and any other diseases, and people who are in difficult life situation and in places of imprisonment.

PRINCIPLES OF CONDUCTION OF ACTIVITIES

Responsibility and servicing for the sake of society:

Monetary funds received from representatives of the society, state and donor organizations should not be used for mercenary purposes. All financial funds are the means of expression of trust and require very attentive attitude.

Everyday improvement:

Constantly developing and introducing new ideas and technologies, aiming at sustainable growth of organization.

Attention to the target groups:

Constantly improving informativeness and skills, offering high-quality services, help the clients of organization, act for their interests, support their trust.

Human rights:

∑Provision of safe conditions for those who works on behalf of organization;

∑Protection of environment;

∑Observance of all acting laws on salary and working hours;

∑Strict prohibition of trafficking of people and use of the children or forced labor

∑Fairness and equity in human relations.

EXPECTATIONS

What is expected from the staff-members:

Conscientious execution of relevant duties and honesty are of the first priority in the activities of the staff-member of the Centre.

In his professional activities the staff-member of the Centre is obliged to observe Constitution of the Republic of Uzbekistan, to be governed with the Laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan, by the Rules of the internal work discipline, other normative legal acts and rules of behavior established by the acting Code.

Staff-members and volunteers support the highest professional standards and level of private behavior, responsibly use information and resources, avoid conflict of interests.

Staff-members abstain from the use of their official position for getting special privileges or other benefit for themselves.

Every staff-member tries to develop and support trust of public, mass media, Government, partners, donor organizations, NGOs, beneficiaries and other staff-members of organization.

Every staff-member demonstrates real social responsibility, transparency and honesty in information which he makes available for public.

Staff-members of organization should keep confidentiality about personal information of staff- members and other persons with the exception of cases when natural persons refuse this right or when disclosure is required by the law.

EXTERNAL INTERACTION

Interaction with donors

Organization guarantees that all grant/project funds are used as promised or envisaged in appeals for collection of money or for the purposes foreseen by donors.

When accepting grant, organization concludes contract/agreement for execution of activities/project according to the before agreed procedures and bears ethic and juridical responsibility for observance of these obligations. Contract/agreement can be changed after getting written consent of donor.

Organization undertakes a commitment for guaranteeing of efficient and prompt use and realization of grants and donations.

Financial accounts on financial expense are submitted according to the donor procedures agreed beforehand.

Directors, administration, staff-members and volunteers of organization should not use any relations with donor or possible donor for self-profit.

Confidential information about donor or donations should not be distributed to unauthorized persons.

Partnership

Organization cooperates with other organizations only if these relations accord to the goals and tasks of organization.

Organization cooperates with other organizations on the base of common values, common positions and for the benefit of society.

Organization cooperates on the base of just and real benefit for each organization.

Organization cooperates with guaranteeing the financial transparency and bilateral flow of information, ideas and experience.

Organization cooperates with other NNOs as means for rising the efficiency and effectiveness of civil society, upgrading the ability to promote social benefit for the sake of interests of the target groups of organization.

Relations with the governmental bodies and Intergovernmental bodies

Organization concludes partner agreements with governmental and/or intergovernmental bodies when it is beneficial for reaching the goals and tasks by organization, rising the efficiency of the projects’ realization and do not threaten the independence or self-control of organization.

DOCUMENTATION

Accuracy of documentation

When the business papers are being prepared, the organization is governed by principles of honesty and accessibility. Everybody is responsible for the comprehensive, honest, accurate, timely and clear information which is contained in business papers including table of work-sheet, reports on expenditures and other documentation.

Belief in the ethic and honest actions requires that organization should take part only in legal and authorized business operations. In no case none of the staff-members should present false information in oral or written form on behalf of organization. Besides, nobody should conceal money funds of organization and falsely characterize transactions of organization, create hidden money accounts or intentionally afford such illegal actions. If there are doubts or concern related to accounting, keeping accounting documentation or audit, then it is required to apply to the members of the Board of the Centre or to the chief accountant of organization.

Collection of documentation

Proper storage and deletion of documents are important aspects of keeping the accurate business information. Staff-members should collect all documentation of organization in accordance with standards fixed in the schedules of collection of documentation, donor requirements, as well as the laws of the Republic of Uzbekistan. These schedules of collection of documentation determine the time of storage of business documents, as well as the ways of their deletion.

It is forbidden to delete, change or hide any documents including electronic files, electronic mail and disks after getting or expecting notifications from the governmental actions of law or legal actions.

Financial documents

Observance of the proper financial procedures, control over finances, accounting and procurements in organization should be organized in such a way that the investors, lawmakers, governmental bodies and public should not be misled in relation to financial aspects of organization. When purchasing the goods and services it is required to follow the due procurement procedures and to have all relevant permissions. The responsible persons should undertake efforts for observance of all applicable laws.

Internal financial accountability should be made on regular basis and submitted to administrative body. All substantial differences between the budget expenditures and actual expenditures should be determined and explained to administrative body.

WORK CONDITIONS

Work environment

Organization appreciates different ideas, opinions and work experience, believes in the culture of acceptance and perception of differences in the ethnic culture. Irrespectively of the right of every staff-member to have own opinions and values, these opinions and values should not be pressed on the other staff-members. This means that the staff-members of organization should not use the rooms and communication facilities of organization (electronic mail, inner corporative network, bulletin-boards, etc.) for propaganda of their religious, political or other delicate personal opinions.

Programs, projects and plans of organization should work in efficient way for reaching statutory goals and tasks of organization.

The staff-members of organization should not implement activities not related to the statutory goals and tasks and/or realized projects during their paid work hours.

None of the net profit or organization should be used in the interests of director, officials, members or staff-members of organization or any other private persons besides reasonable disbursement for services provided by them to organization.

Any excess of financial resources formed in the result of realization of projects and other operations should be exclusively used for supporting organization in execution of the stated goals and tasks.

Statute and Regulations of affiliated branches should be accessible for all parties concerned.

Minutes of every session of administrating body, as well as yearly reports should be prepared and archived for the further use. The minutes should be also accessible for the staff-members of organization, officials, inspecting organizations.

Discrimination

Everybody should work in such a way that the workplace can guarantee the equal conditions of employment and career development, forms of promotion and disciplinary measures for everybody. It is not recommended to make decisions in connection with employment basing on certain characteristics protected by relevant law, for example, age, skin color, gender, gender identification, national origin, physical or psychical disability, race, religion, or any other personal characteristics protected by law. If anybody suspects or feels discrimination in relation to him, then it is necessary to inform the member of the Board of the Centre or to human resources department of organization about this.

Harassment

Harassment is impermissible in organization. Within the framework of this Code harassment is implied as any form of obsessive behavior in regard to another person, which causes unpleasant, hostile or offensive work environment for him. The promises of help in career development or special attitude in exchange for satisfaction of request of sexual character are considered as harassment. Organization does not admit such behavior, regardless of the consideration of this behavior as harassment by the national legislation. If the staff-member was or is subjected to harassment, the he should immediately apply to the Board of the Centre or to human resources department of organization about this.

Confidentiality of the staff-members

Relationships between the staff-members and organization are based on mutual trust and respect. For supporting these relations everybody should make input for saving the personal

information about the staff-members as confidential one. This is executed following the internal control rules of NNO «INTILISH» RIEC and subordinating to the relevant legislation acts of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

This means that without due authorization nobody can get access to any system, data bases or records which contain personal information about potential, present or former staff-members.

Safety and security

All staff-members have a right for the safe and secure workplace. All staff-members should follow all rules and procedures of safety and immediately inform about dangerous work conditions, as well as inform about traumas or diseases to the Board of the Centre or to human resources department of organization about this.

All staff-members should be aware of the plan of actions is case of emergency situations. Is somebody is threaten, it is necessary to act in accord with the plan of organization which Implies instant communication with the local emergency services.

Guaranteeing safety of the workplace implies also whittling the possibility of violation on the territory of organization or in case of conduction of business on behalf of organization. It is not recommended to do the following:

∑Manifest any form of violation on the workplace including threats, threatening behavior, harassment, intimidation, contempt, etc.

∑It is necessary to be vigilant and immediately inform about presence of strangers or about unusual actions which result in robbery of property or its damage or cause injury to the personnel of organization.

In all cases of violation of safety it is necessary to inform the member of the Board of the Centre about this immediately.

Environment and communities

All staff-members are committed to avoid negative impact on the environment including soil, air and water which everybody uses, and on the communities where the staff-members live and work.

In case of necessity it is necessary to inform about all possible problems of environment immediately or apply for the advice regarding the proper observance of the laws and rules of its protection. If it is known or suspected that there is a threat to the environment or if the due standards are violated, then it is necessary to inform the member of the Board of the Centre about this immediately.

PROPERTY

Assets of organization

Assets include material property, intellectual property, information technologies and reputation of organization.

Organization will carry out prompt investigation, and, in the case of necessity, criminal examination of the claimed cases of fraud or robbery of assets. It is necessary to inform about all suspected cases of robbery, losses or improper treating of assets of organization to the members of the Board of the Centre immediately.

Material property

The production of organization, financial resources, rooms, transport facilities and equipment are considered as property. It is necessary to save this valuable property in a proper way and always protect it. Everybody is responsible for using the property of organization only for the benefit of organization.

Intellectual property

Intellectual property of NNO «INTILISH» RIEC is at least as much (if not more) valuable as property. It is necessary to save this property in a proper way and protect it. Intellectual property (IP) includes patents, author's rights and commercial secrets, as well as technical data and software developed in organization. As a rule, organization preserves exclusive right of property for IP in a form of ideas, processes, inventions or upgrades developed during the work in organization. Observance of safety obligation for intellectual property by the staff members acts even when the work of staff-member in organization is terminated.

Information technologies

Information technologies are one of the valuable assets of organization. They should be used only for the proper and reasonably authorized business purposes, i.e., nobody has right to use network systems of organization for downloading of transfer of impropriate, non-professional, provocative or illegal content. This content includes indecent, rude messages with sexual harassment or socially or ethnically offensive messages, photos and video.

As information technologies being used in the work of organization belong to organization, then it should be expected that electronic communication, any activity in internet, as well as voice messages (incoming and outgoing) in organization network are not regarded as personal and confidential ones. Organization preserves right of looking through official electronic mail and activities in internet, and will exercise this right according to legislation of the Republic of Uzbekistan.

Confirmation of improper use of the network systems provided by organization can result in the complete prohibition of access to them and disciplinary measures.

CONFLICT OF INTERESTS

Conflict of interests

Conflict of interests appears when private interests of the staff-member impedes (or are regarded as impeding) the objective decision making in the interests of organization. Evasion from the actual or seeming conflict of interests give a rise to and supports the trust of clients and other business partners and public, that is why it is very important to be vigilant in this field. Though it is impossible to describe every situation when the conflict of interests may occur, the following sections consider the most prevalent of them.

Relations between staff-members

Conflict of interests often emerges in case of personal relations between the staff-members. Even if these relations do not impact making proper, objective business decisions in inadmissible way, the people in surroundings may think that this is not right. This statement is actually right mainly in the case of administrator and subordinated person (direct or indirect), who are romantic partners or relatives. Besides, it is often can be regarded as incorrect if the paid or unpaid work is performed for somebody who is direct or indirect administrator. As a rule it is recommended to avoid such situations. If somebody is in personal relations with anybody, which can be regarded as influencing the decision making, then it is necessary to inform the members of the Board of the Centre or to human resources department of organization about this.

Personal benefit of personal opportunities

When working in organization, it is possible to find out the business opportunities in which there is personal interest. It is prohibited to persecute or give an advice to the third party is persecution of any opportunities which the staff-member got to know in regard to work or when using the property with the exception of cases when the written permission of the Director of Centre is obtained.

Performing of business transactions with relatives

It is necessary to be careful when one of the family members works in the company with which the organization performs or intends to perform business transactions. When you appeared to be in such situation when the work implies also making business decision in regard to that company, then it is necessary to inform members of the Board of the Centre immediately about this.

Investments to other companies

Conflict of interests may arise if the staff-member or the member of the staff-member family has financial interest in a private enterprise with which the organization performs transactions or compete. In such situation the possibility of conflict of interests depends on the amount of investment, role in organization and business relations between organization and other company. Before making such investments it is required to get written permission with approval of human resources department of organization.

Work outside corporation

Staff-members should be convinced that their interests and activities outside organization were not in a conflict with their duties regarding organization. As the work outside organization can create an impression of prejudices and incapability in decision making in the best interest of organization, then nobody can be employed, be consultant or in other way be related to competitor, client or provider of organization without preliminary approval with the Director of the Centre.

Presents

When presenting or getting gifts it is required to consider if this is really relevant. If this is related to sale, looks as excessive or can be perceived as a bribe, then it is recommended not to offer and accept such present of such hospitality services.

CONCLUSION

Organization preserves right to make one-side amendments to this Code or any other regulating documents of organization at any time.
